About ABC Candy

A Toblerone is a Swiss chocolate bar made with honey and almond nougat. Created in 1908 by Theodor Tobler, Toblerone is now available around the world and is instantly recognized thanks to its unique chocolate peak shape and unmistakable packs.

Jean Tobler owned his own confectionery shop in Bern, Switzerland, producing chocolate confectionery from products supplied by other manufactures.
